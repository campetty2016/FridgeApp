BBQ Chicken Pizza

Indulge in the perfect BBQ Chicken Pizza for your family and friends or enjoy as a late night snack just for yourself. Anytime is pizza night with this wonderful handmade pizza recipe.

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350℉/175℃.
  2. Place pizza crust on baking sheet.
  3. Spread BBQ sauce on the crust.
  4. Add toppings to customize your pizza
  5. Bake for 15 minutes until cheese has melted.
